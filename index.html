01 — Who we back The founders

Built differently.

We're not looking for the safest bet on the market. We're looking for the founder who'd build this brand in a recession, in a bull market, in their kitchen if they had to.

The Indian consumer story has been overrun by founders chasing the moment — a hot category, a fundable narrative, a brand that looks good in a pitch deck. Most won't outlast their hype cycle.

We back the other kind. Operators who understand their consumer like a craftsman understands their material. Founders with a point of view so specific it would be dismissed in a board meeting — and so right it becomes obvious in five years.

Builders built for the long arc. The kind you'd bet on twice.

The Firm

Operator-led capital, for founders building something that outlasts trends.

ConsumerX is built by operators. We've launched, scaled and sold consumer brands ourselves — through the messy, unglamorous early days most pitch decks skip over.

So we invest the way we wish we'd been invested in: early, honest, and for the long haul. Less board-seat theatre, more rolling up sleeves on brand, distribution, retail and supply chain — the work that actually compounds.

When we say we write the first cheque and stay through the build, we mean it as a ten-year promise, not a line in a term sheet.
